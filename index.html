Our project aims to create an advanced Chess Game AI, designed not just to challenge expert players but also to help beginners learn. We're building this with a foundation of detailed planning documents and tools: from a Software Requirements Specification (SRS) that outlines what our AI needs to do, to a Software Design Specification (SDS) that sketches how we'll build it. We've laid out our entire process in a clear Project Plan, tracked our progress on GitHub, and will share our journey and results in a comprehensive Final Report and a Final Presentation. This structured approach ensures our AI is both innovative and accessible.
